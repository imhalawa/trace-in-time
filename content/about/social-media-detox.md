I've been on a long journey to distance myself from the toxicity of social media, and I’m envisioning this space as my peaceful refuge—a place where I can share freely with a smaller, more intentional audience who genuinely cares, rather than just passive scrollers.

## Why I Avoid Social Media?

Social media represents the modern-day equivalent of a slavery industry. People become ensnared by forces that provoke feelings like envy, anger, and low self-esteem, leading to impulsive reactions. Constructive discussions often seem impossible without damaging each other's mental well-being.

In essence, I’m striving to maintain a healthy and balanced mind.
