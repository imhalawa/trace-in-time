You're looking at it.

Started from the [Coderon Jekyll theme](https://jekyllthemes.io/theme/coderon-blog-jekyll-theme), then pulled it apart and rebuilt most of it to fit how I actually think and work. Dark mode, RTL Arabic, series, books, music, podcasts, projects, custom plugins — each piece added because I needed it, not because it looked good on a features list.

The goal was a space that feels like me. Not a theme dressed up with content.
